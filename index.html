The Valentine's Proposal

It was the day her beautiful brown eyes became mine

The day her sparking, glistening eyes locked with mine

The day I gave her our ring

Finally after 15 years since I last said goodbye

She finally looked into my eyes

The day I no longer heard Goodbye

But a whisper

On this very day......



"Would you like to continue this dance with me?"

"Yes"
